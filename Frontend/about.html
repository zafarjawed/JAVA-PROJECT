About GlowUp 🌸

Hey! I'm Poojashree and Zafar Jawed, a first-year BCA student who’s just starting out in web development. GlowUp is my first full-stack project — and I created it not only for my subject but also because I genuinely love skincare and design 💖

GlowUp is a cute, beginner-friendly skincare app where you can take a simple quiz and get your own personalized skincare routine based on your skin type and concerns like acne, dryness, or dullness. It also gives you daily glow-up tips to stay consistent ✨

I wanted the app to feel relaxing, aesthetic, and useful — something that makes you feel good while learning about self-care. I also challenged myself to build everything from scratch, using only HTML, CSS, and JavaScript (no libraries!) — and I’m really proud of how it turned out 💅

My goal was to mix my creative side with what I’m learning in tech. And honestly, I had so much fun building this — and learned a LOT along the way. I hope people enjoy using it as much as I enjoyed making it 🌷
